## What is Banana Slides?

Banana Slides is an AI-native PPT generation application that creates complete presentations from ideas, outlines, or page descriptions. Powered by advanced image generation models, it produces visually stunning slides with consistent style and accurate text rendering.

## Who is it for?

- **Beginners** — Zero design experience needed, generate beautiful PPTs instantly
- **Professionals** — Get layout and design inspiration from AI-generated compositions
- **Educators** — Quickly convert teaching content into illustrated lesson slides
- **Students** — Focus on content, let AI handle the formatting
- **Business users** — Rapidly visualize proposals and product introductions
